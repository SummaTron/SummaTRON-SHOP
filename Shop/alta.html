How to register a product?

The registration process does not need registration, just complete the fields that are requested in the form:

- Type of product
- Price.
- The TRX account where you will receive the collections.
- The title to show
- A description of 1,024 characters maximum
- URL of the image to show or if you upload a file
- URL of the product to download or upload a file


The TRX account will be the key to access in the future

We assume that the person who includes the product is the owner of the copyright.

How to modify a product

Since in Shop there is no record, and that we are fervent believers in TRON, for modify a product You must access and you are only required to pay 1 TRX from the owner account (the one included in the registration of the product).

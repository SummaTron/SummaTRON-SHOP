What is a NFT?

NFT stands for "non-fungible token," and it can technically contain anything digital, including pictures, animated GIFs, songs, video game items, or ebooks. An NFT can be unique, like a real life painting, or a copy of many, like trading cards, but the blockchain keeps track of who owns the file. An NFT needs to be stored on a blockchain, and the best is TRON.NETWORK
